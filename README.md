# Myntra-Brand-Analytics-Power-bi

# Project Overview
Comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing Myntra's brand performance, product pricings and ratings of customers using Data Cleaning, Power Query, DAX calculations, dynamic visualizations for decision making.
# Objectives

- Helps in analyze brand-wise product performance.
- Also Compares average product prices across brands.
- Evaluate customer ratings.
- Identify product distribution by price range.
- Analyze total brands, total products using KPI cards.
- visualize rating wise top brands.

# Power BI Implementation

1. Measures (DAX calculations)
   
- Average  Price
- Average Rating
- Total Brands
- Total Colors
- Total Products

2. Filters Applied

  Filtered the data using
Brand Name
Color
Price Range
Rating Category
  
3. Visualizations used

📊 Top 10 Brands by Average Price (Bar Chart)
Displays the top 10 brands with the highest average product prices.

🍩 Products by Price Range (Donut Chart)
Shows the distribution of products across different price segments such as Premium, Mid Range, and Budget.

📈 Products by Rating Category (Column Chart)
Categorizes products into Average, Good, and Excellent rating groups.

📦 Top 10 Brands by Product Count (Column Chart)
Displays the top 10 brands with the highest number of products listed on Myntra.

⭐ Top 10 Brands by Average Ratings (Horizontal Bar Chart)
Ranks the top-performing brands based on their average customer ratings.

4. KPI Cards
- Total Brands
- Average Price
- Total Products
- Average Rating

5. Tools and Technologies
  Data Cleaning
  Data Modelling
  Power Query
  Power BI
  DAX
